
'Top-of-the funnel', 'Middle-of-the-funnel' and 'Bottom-of-the-funnel' respectively

- **ToFu** -> brand awareness
	- i.e: a short video promoting good meeting ettiquette on LinkedIn
- **MoFu** -> Customers are actively seeking a solution. Business positioning is important
	- Promote case studies
	- Promote work with existing clients
	- Raise testimonials
- **BoFu** -> An explicit request for the persons' business, a.k.a: CTAs
	- Online Shop -> 'add to cart'
	- consultancy -> 'request a callback'
	- news outlet -> 'register for newsletter'
