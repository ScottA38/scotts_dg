- (known) Life: 1920-1996
- Tulku Rimpoche born in the 'Eastern Tibetan province of Kham'

- Tulku Urgyen Rimpoche's legacy ultimately created a bridge between two Tibetan Lama lineages - the 'Kagyu' and 'Nyingma' lineages


- Lived (and practised) at Nagi Gompa, Nepal
- Key transmissions of teachings:
	- _Chokling Tersar_
	- The 'pointing-out' instruction
		- The 'pointing-out' instruction is dependent upon a students' Root Guru directly 'pointing-out' a facet of the nature of the mind, so that a disciple can digest it. 
- Considered one of the great mediation masters of his time
- Taught meditation to a great diversity of people

#### Key snippets of Tulku Rimpoche's teaching approach
- Unique approach to starting seminar teachings
	- Began by explaining (?) teachings on the view of ultimate reality
	- Tulku Urgyen Rimpoche would then follow on from here by guiding his students to train in wisdom and compassion as expressions of the mind's true nature of _luminous purity_
