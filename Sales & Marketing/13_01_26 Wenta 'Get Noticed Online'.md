**Repeat customers are cheaper and easier for your bank balance!**

Primary effort on Social Media is a viable marketing strategy

- When writing your own website you are telling your story
- You are positioning your brand
- You are showcasing your brand

People will likely disengage from active consideration of your product as soon as browse away from the website

- Go mobile first!

**Build a multi-channel presence** to build legitimacy
