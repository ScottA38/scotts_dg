## Performance

- [**Wall Time**](https://www.techtarget.com/whatis/definition/wall-time-real-world-time-or-wall-clock-time)
	- An abbreviation of 'Wall-Clock time'
	- Defines the amount of time that a computer takes to execute a process based upon a seperate clock sitting alongside the computer. As compared with CPU time, which may be significantly smaller due to context switching (user perceives 7 secs, a.k.a wall time, vs. computer recording 1 sec, a.k.a CPU time, as other processes intervened)
