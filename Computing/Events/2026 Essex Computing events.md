### Chelmsford Science Festival - 'Future Worlds: Earth, Space and You'
- **Host**: Anglia Ruskin University
- **Dates**: 20-28th October 2026
- **Event theme**: 'Future Worlds: Earth, Space and You'
- **Link**: [Info](https://www.aru.ac.uk/events/chelmsford-science-festival)

The programme of the Chelmsford Science Festival seeks to combine the passions of technology enthusiasts young and old into one all-encompassing programme suitable for people of a variety of capabilities and walks of life.

Hosted within the ARU campus, Chelmsford, the Chelmsford Science festival has a huge range of highly-inclusive activities to choose from with participating partner organsiations such as 'the Witches of Chelmsford' and 'Chelmsford For You'.

With ARU Chelmsford providing a high-calibre facility for the event, family-friendly fun is in store for all ages with accommodation for a seperate, family-friendly itinerary on the 24th-25th October (nested tightly within the main event's action-packed schedule).
